Japan

A country that is on top of my travel bucket list

Activities

Tokyo

Kyoto is Japan’s timeless heart, where ancient temples, tranquil gardens, and lantern-lit alleyways blend seamlessly with modern life. A must-do experience here is exploring the enchanting Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its endless rows of vermilion torii gates that wind up the sacred Mount Inari. Walking through this glowing tunnel feels like stepping into another world—equal parts spiritual journey and breathtaking photo opportunity. Whether you linger in the serene hush of the forested trails or capture the iconic gates at golden hour, this is the essence of Kyoto you’ll never forget.
